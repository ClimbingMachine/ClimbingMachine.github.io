About Me

I am a fourth year Ph.D. Candidate in Lyles School of Civil Engineering at Purdue University , supervised by Dr. Jon Fricker .

Prior to entering Purdue, I got my Bachelor's degree in Traffic Engineering from Jilin University.

My research focuses on pedestrian dynamics, human motion prediction, and imitation learning. I am also interested in applications of reinforcement learning algorithms on real world problems, especially for urban computing.
